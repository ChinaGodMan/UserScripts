# 🎧 YouTube Pro Audio Enhancer

one for **YouTube player**Advanced audio enhancement scripts created by，based on **Web Audio API**，Can enhance the low-frequency intensity of video with one click、Vocal clarity and overall loudness，Let ordinary videos have a more professional listening experience。

---

## ✨ Features

- 🔊 **Low frequency enhancement（Sub Bass）**  
  use Low Shelf Filter enhancement approx. 60Hz low frequency，Give music a deeper sense of depth。

- 🧹 **Turbid band reduction（Mud Cut）**  
  exist 250Hz Make cuts nearby，Reduce feeling of stuffiness，Make the sound cleaner。

- 🎤 **Improved vocal clarity（Presence）**  
  promote 2.5kHz sense of presence，Make vocals stand out more、clearer。

- ✨ **High frequency brightness enhancement（Brilliance）**  
  Enhance 8kHz High frequency above，Make the overall listening experience more transparent。

- 📈 **dynamic compression（Compressor）**  
  Balance volume dynamics，Reduce big and small problems，More stable hearing。

- 🔉 **overall gain increase（Gain）**  
  Without obvious distortion，Moderately increase overall volume。

---

## 🖱 Usage

- exist YouTube A new one will be added in the lower left corner of the player **🎵 audio boost button**
- Just click the button **turn on / Turn off audio enhancement**
- The button turns blue to indicate that enhancements are enabled for the current video

---

## 💾 state memory

- The script will automatically record your switch status（`localStorage`）
- After refreshing the page or switching videos，Will automatically restore the last open state

---

## 🔧 Technical implementation

- use `AudioContext` Create an audio processing environment  
- pass `createMediaElementSource()` take over `<video>` audio stream  
- Use multiple `BiquadFilterNode` + `DynamicsCompressorNode` Build an audio processing chain  
- With the help of `MutationObserver` and `yt-navigate-finish` event，adaptation YouTube of SPA Page switching

---

## ⚠ Things to note

- Initial activation may require **After playing the video** To activate the audio context（Browser restrictions）
- different devices / earphone / Speaker effects will vary
- This script only takes effect locally，No audio data will be modified or uploaded

---

## 🚀 Applicable scenarios

- 🎶 music MV / live performance
- 🎧 podcast / Interview / Tutorial
- 📺 The volume is too low or the sound quality is average YouTube video
- 💻 Laptops with weak external sound quality
